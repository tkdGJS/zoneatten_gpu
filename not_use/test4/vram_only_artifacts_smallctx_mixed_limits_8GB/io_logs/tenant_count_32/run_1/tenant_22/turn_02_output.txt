Repudiation can be done by:

* Breaching the contract
* Intentionally refusing to perform
* Failing to perform
* Failing to pay

Repudiation can be communicated through various means, such as:

* Written communication
* Verbal communication
* Email
* Phone calls
* In-person meetings

Repudiation is considered a breach of contract and can lead to:

* Termination of the contract
* Damages to the other party
* Dispute resolution
* Dismissal of the contract
