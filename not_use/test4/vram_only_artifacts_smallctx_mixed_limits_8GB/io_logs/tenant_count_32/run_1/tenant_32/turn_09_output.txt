The owner has the right to dispute a builder’s extension of time for various reasons, including

* Inaccurate or misleading information provided by the builder
* Failure to provide written notice of the extension of time
* Failure to meet the agreed upon time for completion
* Unforeseen circumstances beyond the builder’s control

If the owner disputes the extension of time, the dispute will be referred to the Building Industry Fair Trading (BIT) and the decision will be made by the Building Industry Fair Trading Authority (BIT).
