What is the Defects Liability Period in Building Contracts in Australia?

The Defects Liability Period is the time period during which the contractor is liable to compensate the owner for any defects or defects in the work. * The contractor is responsible for repairing or repairing any defects in the work that are discovered during the course of the project.
* The contractor may be liable for damages to the owner for any defects that are discovered after the completion of the project, but not before.
* The contractor may be liable for damages to the owner for any defects that are caused by the owner's own negligence or failure to comply with the contract.
