What are the consequences of repudiation in a contract?

Repudiation of a contract can have significant consequences for both parties involved. The consequences include:

*   Termination of the contract
*   Loss of any claims for damages
*   Loss of any other remedies available under the contract
*   Damage to their reputation
*   Potential legal action
