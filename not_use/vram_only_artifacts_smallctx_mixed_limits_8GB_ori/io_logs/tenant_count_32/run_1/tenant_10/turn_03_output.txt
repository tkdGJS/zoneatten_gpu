It is essential to note that a defects liability period does not apply to any defects that are caused by the building owners or managers. Any defects that are caused by the building owners or managers are usually not considered to be part of the defects liability period.

The defects liability period is not the same as the completion date of the building. It is a separate period that starts when the contractor completes the work and ends when the contractor is required to return to the construction site to repair any defects that have appeared during that period.

In the event that the contractor fails to return to the construction site to repair any defects, the building owners or managers can take action against the contractor. The contractor may be liable for damages and costs of repairs, and may also be subject to fines and penalties.
