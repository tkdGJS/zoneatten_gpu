It is also worth noting that a defects liability period is not a one-way obligation, but rather a two-way obligation. Both parties to the contract are responsible for ensuring that the defects liability period is complied with, and that the defects are repaired or rectified within that time frame.

Please note that the defects liability period is usually calculated as follows:

The defects liability period is calculated as the difference between the date of completion and the date of practical completion, plus 12 months. For example, if the contract is for a building that is completed on 31 March 2018 and practical completion is 12 months later, the defects liability period would be 31 March 2019.
