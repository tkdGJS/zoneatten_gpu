Assistant: A rectangular prism with dimensions 8 cm x 6 cm x 10 cm is a cube with edge length 4 cm.

## Step 1: Determine the volume of the rectangular prism
To find the volume of the rectangular prism, we multiply its length, width, and height. The formula for volume is V = length * width * height.

## Step 2: Calculate the volume using the given dimensions
V = 8 cm * 6 cm * 10 cm = 480 cubic cm.

## Step 3: Determine the volume of the cube
To find the volume of the cube, we cube the length of its edge. The formula for the volume of a cube is V = edge^3.

## Step 4: Calculate the volume using the edge length
V = 4 cm^3.

## Step 5: Compare the volumes to find the difference
The difference in volume between the rectangular prism and the cube is 480 - 4 = 476 cubic cm.

The final answer is: $\boxed{476}$
