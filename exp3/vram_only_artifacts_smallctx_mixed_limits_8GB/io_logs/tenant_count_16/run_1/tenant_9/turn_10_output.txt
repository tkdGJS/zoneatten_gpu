The length of the defects liability period can be specified in the contract or can be determined by reference to the building codes and standards that were used in the construction of the works.

In many cases, the defects liability period will be a fixed number of months, such as 12, 18 or 24 months. However, it is not uncommon for contracts to include a clause that allows for the defects liability period to be extended by agreement between the parties.
