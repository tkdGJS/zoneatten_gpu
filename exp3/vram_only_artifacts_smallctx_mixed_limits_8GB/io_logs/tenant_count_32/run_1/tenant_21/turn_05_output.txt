Repudiation can lead to termination of the contract. In some cases, the innocent party may be able to terminate the contract through contractual provisions or the common law right of repudiation.

However, repudiation can also lead to termination through the common law right of repudiation. In such circumstances, the innocent party may seek damages for any losses suffered.
