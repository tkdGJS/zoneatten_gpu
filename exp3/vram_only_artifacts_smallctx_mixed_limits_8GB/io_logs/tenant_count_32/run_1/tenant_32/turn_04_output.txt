What is an Extension of Time Claim?

An Extension of Time Claim is a formal request to the builder to extend the completion date of the construction project due to unforeseen circumstances.

The builder must provide written notice of the claim to the homeowner within the required timeframe. The homeowner can contest the claim if they disagree with the builder’s reasons.

What is the purpose of an Extension of Time Claim?

The purpose of an Extension of Time Claim is to:

* Protect the homeowner’s interests by ensuring the construction project stays on track
* Ensure the builder’s responsibilities are clear
* Provide a fair resolution to the dispute
