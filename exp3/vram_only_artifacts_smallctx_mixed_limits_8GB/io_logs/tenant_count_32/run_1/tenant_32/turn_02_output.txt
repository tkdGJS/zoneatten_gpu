Understanding The Rights as a Homeowner

Provide written notice of dispute: If the homeowner disagrees with the builder’s claim for an extension of time, the homeowner must provide written notice of dispute within the reasonable timeframe of receiving the builder’s notice. This notice should detail the reasons why the homeowner is disputing the Extension of Time Claim.
