Repudiation can be caused by a variety of factors such as:

* A material change in circumstances that makes it impossible for the party to fulfill their obligations
* A material change in circumstances that makes the contract impracticable to perform
* A material breach of a term of the contract
* A material breach of a duty to perform

Repudiation of a contract is not the same as termination. It is a breach of contract, and the other party may not be able to terminate the contract.
