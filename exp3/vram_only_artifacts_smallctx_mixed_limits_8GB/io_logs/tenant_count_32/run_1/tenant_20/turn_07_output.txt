Repudiation can be accepted by a party to a contract by:

* Not fulfilling their obligations under the contract
* Not fulfilling their obligations to the other party
* Not fulfilling their obligations to the other party in a material breach of a term of the contract

Assistant: Key Points:

* When a party accepts repudiation, the innocent party has the right to terminate the contract
* The termination results in the contract being deemed void at common law
* Damages may be available in a claim for breach of contract
* Accepting repudiation does not imply that the repudiating party has waived their right to terminate the contract
