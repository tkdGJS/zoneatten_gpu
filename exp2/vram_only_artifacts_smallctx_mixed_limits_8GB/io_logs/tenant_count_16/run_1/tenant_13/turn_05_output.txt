In general, a new defects liability period will not apply to rectified work, unless the principal has agreed to the rectification work and the rectification work is carried out by a different contractor.

However, the principal may agree to the rectification work and the rectification work may be carried out by a different contractor.

In such cases, the rectification work may be included in the defects liability period of the original contract.

Assistant: Assistant: Once a defect is fixed, a new defects liability period will not apply. The rectification work is considered to be completed and the original defects liability period will not be restarted. The contractor can continue to work on the project and the client can continue to pay for the original defects liability period.
