What are the benefits of specifying a defects liability period in a contract?

The benefits of specifying a defects liability period in a contract include:

* Providing certainty as to the process that will be followed for making good any defects.
* Providing a means of making good any defects that are apparent at the time of practical completion.
* Making it easier to identify and rectify defects that are not immediately apparent.
* Reducing the risk of disputes between principals and contractors.
* Providing a framework for the contractor to rectify defects within a given period.
