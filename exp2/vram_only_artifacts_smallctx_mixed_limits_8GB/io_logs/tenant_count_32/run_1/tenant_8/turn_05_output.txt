However, if the contractor is the one who rectifies the defect, then the defects liability period will continue until the completion of the rectified work, even though the defect may have been rectified.

It is also worth noting that the rectified work may be considered as part of the principal's obligations under the contract, and therefore may be subject to the same defects liability period.
