Assistant: Repudiation and termination are distinct concepts. Repudiation is the conduct or event that brings about the termination of the contract, while termination is the actual act of bringing an end to the contract.

Repudiation does not necessarily require a breach of contract. It can be caused by material breach, mental incapacity, or personal insolvency.

The innocent party may not have a contractual right to terminate the contract. In such circumstances, they may rely on the common law right of repudiation to terminate the contract.
