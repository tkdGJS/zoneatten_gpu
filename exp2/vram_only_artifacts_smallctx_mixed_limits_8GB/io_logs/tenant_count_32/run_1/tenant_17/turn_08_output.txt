If you have a contract with a repudiatory breach, you can bring a claim for damages for breach of contract. The damages may include:

1.  **Loss of payment**: The innocent party may be entitled to recover the amount paid to the party whose conduct is repudiatory.
2.  **Loss of opportunity cost**: The innocent party may be able to recover the opportunity cost of the work that was performed by the party whose conduct is repudiatory.
3.  **Loss of goodwill**: The innocent party may be able to recover the loss of goodwill resulting from the repudiatory conduct.
