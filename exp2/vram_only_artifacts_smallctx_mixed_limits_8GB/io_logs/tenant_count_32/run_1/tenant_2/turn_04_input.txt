Human: You reach for the cupboard door handle, but when you pull on it, it doesn't budge. It doesn't feel locked. More like... sealed. Like there's some magic at play that you don't understand. You turn to leave it for now, but before you can move away, you feel a strange sensation on the back of your neck. It's warm, scratchy, and wet. You whirl back around, and aaagh! There's a tongue where the cupboard door used to be! It's almost as big as you, a shocking purple, and it's attempting to lick you! Magic you don't understand, indeed.
Just to make things worse, the tongue curls over itself, and actually finds a way to speak to you. “Mmm, thirsty,” it gurgles. “Favourite drinkssss.”
You take a step back, and thankfully, it doesn't follow you. It's stuck to the cupboard.
