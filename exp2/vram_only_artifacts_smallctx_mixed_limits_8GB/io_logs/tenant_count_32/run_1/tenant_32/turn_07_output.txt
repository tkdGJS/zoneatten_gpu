Payment Schedule

* The payment schedule should outline the payment amounts and payment periods for each stage of the construction project.
* The payment schedule should be provided to the homeowner in writing, at least 14 days before the payment is due.
* The payment schedule should include the payment amounts and payment periods for each stage of the construction project, including the commencement of work, time for completion, and completion of work.
* The payment schedule should also include the payment amounts and payment periods for any outstanding amounts.

Contract Terms and Conditions

* The contract terms and conditions should be provided to the homeowner, at least 14 days before the payment is due.
* The contract terms and conditions should be clear and transparent, outlining the rights and obligations of both the homeowner and the builder.
