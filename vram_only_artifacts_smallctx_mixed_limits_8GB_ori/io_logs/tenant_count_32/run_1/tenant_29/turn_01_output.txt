## Step 1: Understanding the concept of human capital
Human capital refers to the skills, knowledge, and abilities of individuals within a population, which can be acquired through education, experience, and training. It is a measure of the value of human labor and the productivity that it brings to the economy.

## Step 2: Identifying the relationship between human capital and economic growth
Human capital is a key driver of economic growth because it enables individuals to contribute to the economy through their labor, innovation, and entrepreneurship. As human capital increases, it can lead to increased productivity, innovation, and economic growth.
