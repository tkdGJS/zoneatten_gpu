A defects liability period gives both principals and contractors a degree of certainty as to the process that will be followed for making good any defects which may not be apparent at the date of practical completion.

Wherever possible, it also makes practical sense to have the contractor who carried out the original work return to fix any defect as this contractor will be familiar with the site and the work in question. This is likely the most cost effective approach to any rectification work.
