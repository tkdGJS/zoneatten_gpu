The defects liability period is designed to provide a degree of certainty as to the process that will be followed for making good any defects which may not be apparent at the date of practical completion.

The purpose of the defects liability period is to protect both the Contractor and the Client from excessive claims and to encourage contractors to take responsibility for the quality of their work.

Defects liability periods can vary in length and can range from 6 months to 3 years.
