The defects liability period is intended to provide a degree of certainty as to the process that will be followed for making good any defects which may not be apparent at the date of practical completion. The period is usually specified in the contract and can vary depending on the type of contract, the type of work, and the nature of the defects.

The defects liability period may also be used to protect the consumer's rights and interests in the building project.
