In effect, the defects liability obligation recognises that defects will arise in the period following completion and includes the obligation to repair those defects in the general obligation to complete the works to the required standard.

The defects liability period provides a practical mechanism to the Employer for the repair or making good of defects which may not be apparent before completion, without resorting to dispute resolution.
