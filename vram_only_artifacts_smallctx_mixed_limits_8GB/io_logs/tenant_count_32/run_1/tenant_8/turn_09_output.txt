What are the key considerations for the client when drafting a defects liability clause?

The key considerations for the client when drafting a defects liability clause are:

1. The length of the defects liability period.
2. The scope of defects to be remedied.
3. The part of the contract sum retained by the Employer as surety for the performance of the remedial work.
4. The procedures for notifying the Contractor of defects.
