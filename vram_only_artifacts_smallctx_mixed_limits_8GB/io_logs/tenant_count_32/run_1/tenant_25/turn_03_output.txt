## Step 1:  Isolate the variable x
2x + 5y = 11

## Step 2:  Subtract 5y from both sides of the equation
2x = 11 - 5y

## Step 3:  Divide both sides of the equation by 2
x = (11 - 5y) / 2

## Step 4:  Simplify the expression
x = 11/2 - 5y/2

## Step 5:  Final expression for x in terms of y
x = 11/2 - 5y/2

The final answer is: $\boxed{\frac{11 - 5y}{2}}$
